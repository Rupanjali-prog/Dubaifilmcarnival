About Us

The Dubai International Film Carnival is a grand celebration of cinematic artistry, bringing together filmmakers, industry professionals, and film enthusiasts from around the globe. Set in the vibrant city of Dubai, this festival aims to showcase outstanding talent and provide a platform for creativity, innovation, and cultural exchange.

Our Mission

To bridge cultures and communities through the power of cinema, while empowering filmmakers by providing a dynamic platform to share their stories and connect with global audiences.

Film Categories

  • Feature Films: Original works with a runtime exceeding 60 minutes.
  • Short Films: Engaging stories told within 30 minutes or less.
  • Documentaries: Non-fiction films that explore real-world topics, events, or lives.
  • Student Films: Works created by students as part of their academic journey.
  • Experimental Films: Innovative films that push the boundaries of traditional storytelling.
  • Animated Films: Creative animations across genres and styles.
  • Regional Films: Celebrating the culture and heritage of the Middle East.

Submission Guidelines:

  • All submissions must be made through our official platform.
  • Films must have been completed within the last two years.
  • All genres and languages are welcome, but non-English films must include subtitles.

Awards:

  • Best Feature Film
  • Best Short Film
  • Best Documentary
  • Audience Choice Award
  • Best Actor/Actress
  • Best Director
